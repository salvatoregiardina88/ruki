RUKI PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: 21 June 2026 - App version: 0.1.0
Data controller: Salvatore Giardina - contact: salvatoregiardina88@gmail.com


1. IN SHORT

Ruki is an application that runs entirely on your computer. The maker of Ruki does not collect,
does not receive and does not store your data: there are no Ruki servers. Your data stays on your
device, except for what - in order to provide its AI features - is sent to Google Gemini using your
own Google key/account, plus a minimal update check to GitHub.


2. DATA PROCESSED AND WHERE IT STAYS (on your device)

Everything under your user folder (%APPDATA%\Ruki):
- Google Gemini API key: stored encrypted (Windows DPAPI, tied to your Windows account) and used
  only to authenticate you with Google. It never leaves your PC except as an authentication header
  to Google.
- App settings.
- Learned memories and user profile (local database).
- Teaching sessions: screen (images/video), microphone audio, PC events (clicks, typed text, window
  changes) and the session's chat messages.
- Technical logs and the count of tokens used (for the cost estimate).


3. DATA SENT TO THIRD PARTIES

- Google Gemini (Google). To reply in chat, learn from sessions and perform tasks, Ruki sends to
  Google Gemini - using your key/your account - the chat messages, screenshots/recordings, audio,
  events and relevant memories. This processing happens within YOUR relationship with Google:
  Google's privacy policy and the Gemini API terms apply. Note: on the free tier Google may use the
  data to improve its products; on the paid tier it generally does not. Refer to Google's official
  pages for privacy and pricing.
- GitHub (update check). At startup Ruki may check for a new version by querying the public releases
  on GitHub: this is a normal web request (which, like any connection, involves sending your IP
  address to GitHub). No personal data is sent intentionally; the feature can be disabled in the
  settings.

Ruki uses no analytics/telemetry, sends no data to the maker, and sells data to no one.


4. PASSWORD FIELDS

During teaching, Ruki masks password fields (detected via Windows accessibility): characters typed
into passwords are not recorded or sent. Due to technical limits, coverage is not guaranteed to be
100% (some applications do not expose this information): avoid typing highly sensitive credentials
during teaching.


5. RETENTION AND YOUR CONTROL

- You can delete memories and profile from the Memory section, discard an ongoing recording (the
  session's data is deleted) and remove the API key from the settings.
- Old sessions are deleted automatically beyond a configurable number.
- Uninstalling removes the application; the data in your user folder remains on your PC and you can
  delete it manually.


6. USER RESPONSIBILITY

You are responsible for what you show and record with Ruki. Do not train Ruki on confidential or
personal data of third parties without being entitled to do so.


7. MINORS

Ruki is not intended for children under 14.


8. YOUR RIGHTS

Because the data is local and/or processed through your own Google account, you can review and
delete it directly on your device. For data processed by Google, exercise your rights (access,
deletion, etc.) with Google. For questions about this policy, write to
salvatoregiardina88@gmail.com.
